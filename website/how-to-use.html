How to Use CricSynthesis

Complete guide to getting started with AI-powered fantasy cricket

Getting Started

CricSynthesis makes it easy to create winning fantasy cricket teams using machine learning. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Upload Your Dataset

Navigate to the Data Ingestion page and upload a cricket ball-by-ball CSV file in Cricsheet format. Your dataset should include:

  • match_id - Unique match identifier
  • batting_team - Team batting
  • bowling_team - Team bowling
  • striker - Batsman on strike
  • bowler - Bowler
  • runs_off_bat - Runs scored
  • extras - Extra runs
  • venue - Ground name

Step 2: Train or Load a Model

You have two options:

  • Train a new model: Go to Model Training and select your training scope (complete dataset or recent matches only)
  • Load existing model: Click "Model Library" in the sidebar and select a previously saved model

Note: Models are automatically saved to the library after training completes.

Step 3: Configure Your Match

On the Squad Configuration page:

  1. Select Team 1 from the dropdown
  2. Select Team 2 from the dropdown
  3. Choose the venue where the match will be played

Step 4: Select Players

Go to Roster Management and build your squad:

  • Search for players using the search box
  • Add players to your selection (minimum 1 player required)
  • Assign each player to their respective team
  • Remove players if needed

Step 5: Get Predictions

Navigate to Performance Forecast to see:

  • AI-predicted fantasy points for each player
  • Sorted rankings by predicted performance
  • Captain and vice-captain recommendations
  • Optimal 11-player lineup

Tips for Best Results

  • Use complete datasets: More data = better predictions
  • Train on recent matches: For quick testing, use recent matches only (20-50 matches)
  • Save your models: Build a library of models for different leagues and tournaments
  • Consider context: Venue and opposition matter! The AI considers these factors
  • Don't panic if players are missing: New/debutant players won't be in historical data - select who you can
